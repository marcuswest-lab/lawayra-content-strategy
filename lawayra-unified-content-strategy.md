## Content Formats (Team's 8 Formats)

### 1. Weekly Group Memory Reel (Every Saturday)
- Highlight reel of that week's guest group
- Add all guests as Instagram collaborators
- Collect Instagram handles → CRM
- Ask for review before departure → post review as comment
- Incentive: LaWayra bracelet or t-shirt

### 2. Vibe Content (Emotional Branding)
- Music sessions (live medicine music, ceremony songs)
- Atmospheric footage: nature, altars, fire, morning mists, sunset
- Short, cinematic, minimal text — let the visuals speak
- Trending audio when appropriate

### 3. Storytelling: Day in the Life
- Morning rituals, mealtimes, personal reflection
- Connection & community time
- Ceremony prep and integration practices
- Both guest and volunteer perspectives

### 4. Team & Volunteer Intros + BTS (Bi-weekly)
- "Meet the Team" series — 1 person per post
- Behind-the-scenes reels: teamwork, laughter, daily life
- Carousel: "5 reasons people love volunteering at LaWayra"
- Volunteer testimonials
- Roles: kitchen, remote help, operations, ceremony facilitation

### 5. Testimonials
- Collected before departure (Sunday)
- Shared as carousels, reels, or stories
- Paired with visuals of their time at LaWayra
- Review posted again in comments for visibility
- Filming SOP: portrait mode, 2-3 min max, quiet space, good lighting
- 6 questions: experience, intention, ceremony, before feeling, after feeling, follow-up

### 6. Community & Inclusivity
- LaWayra Community platform (Circle)
- Preparation Course as onboarding
- LGBTQI+ inclusive, all genders, all backgrounds welcome
- Celebrate diversity in the circle

### 7. Educational Content on Ayahuasca
- What to do during a challenging experience ("We've got you")
- Myths around purging (energetic release, not just physical)
- Types of release: crying, shaking, yawning, sweating
- Somatic tools used during integration
- **Hormozi hooks fit HERE** — our 55-hook bank serves this pillar

### 8. FAQ & Myth-Busting Series ("Real Talk")
- Format: carousel, talking-head reel, quote overlay, story highlight
- 30+ questions organized by category:

**Physical:** Is it safe? What's purging? Physical side effects?
**Mental/Emotional:** Can it trigger psychosis? Mental health conditions? Addiction? Do I need to be spiritual?
**The Ceremony:** Can I trust the facilitators? Will I be in control? What if I panic? How long does it last?
**General:** What IS ayahuasca? How is it different from mushrooms/LSD? Will I hallucinate?
**Prep & Safety:** What's the dieta? How do I prepare mentally? Medical history importance? Is the environment safe?

Our 55-hook bank and Hook > Meat > CTA framework serve the team's plan as **tools within their pillars**, not as a separate strategy.

| Hook Category | Maps to Team Pillar |
|--------------|---------------------|
| Command hooks ("Watch this before...") | What We Do (Educational) |
| Statement hooks — educational | What We Do (Educational) |
| Statement hooks — contrarian | What We Do (FAQ/Myth-Busting) |

## Filming Protocols (from Team SOP)

### Consent
- Head Facilitator asks guests at tour start if they're okay being filmed
- Never film guests during ceremony (Taita/shaman only)
- Respect anyone who declines

### Testimonial Collection (Sunday)
- Quiet space, good lighting, natural background
- Portrait mode, 2-3 min max
- Offer incentive (bracelet or t-shirt)
- 6 questions: experience, intention, ceremony, before/after feelings, follow-up
- Get explicit consent for social media use

### Weekly Group Reel (Saturday)
- Edit highlight reel by Saturday evening
- Add guests as Instagram collaborators
- Collect handles → CRM
- Share Sunday before departure
