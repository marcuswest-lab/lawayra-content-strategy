Application Rules

The small decisions that keep the brand feeling consistent across web, social, and print.

  • CTA buttons: Amber (#E4A017) background, white text, fully rounded (border-radius: 40px). No other color gets a rounded-pill treatment.
  • Section background rotation: cream → olive → beige → cream. Alternating tones create rhythm down long pages.
  • Headings: Always lowercase, Poppins SemiBold or Bold. Never all-caps.
  • Body text: Poppins Regular, Charcoal (#2B2728) on light backgrounds, Cream (#FFF7E7) on dark.
  • Grain textures: Used as subtle overlays on hero images — never full opacity.
  • Photography: Warm, natural, analog film feel. Mountains, jungle paths, ceremonies, team moments. No stock-photo vibes.
  • Voice: Warm friend who has walked this path. "sacred medicine" not "drug", "guests" not "clients", "healing journey" not "program".
