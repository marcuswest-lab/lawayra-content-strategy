# LaWayra - Instagram Content Strategy Project Brief

## The Brand

**LaWayra** is an ayahuasca retreat center located near Medellin, Colombia in the Fredonia region. The retreat offers authentic ayahuasca ceremonies led by indigenous shamans, with a focus on healing and personal transformation. With 2,500+ lives transformed and 500+ 5-star reviews, LaWayra positions itself as the "#1 Aya Retreat in South America."

**What makes LaWayra different:**
- Authentic indigenous shaman-led ceremonies (not a Westernized wellness spa)
- Affordable entry point ($545+) compared to premium retreats like Rhythmia
- Located in Colombia - growing destination for plant medicine tourism
- Strong review track record across multiple platforms
- Gabor Mate association/content (credibility in the trauma-healing space)
- Comprehensive support: preparation, ceremony, and integration

## Marketing Goals

| Priority | Goal | What Success Looks Like |
|----------|------|------------------------|
| 1 | Drive retreat bookings | Instagram content directly leads to inquiries and bookings |
| 2 | Build trust & community | Followers feel connected, educated, and confident in choosing LaWayra |
| 3 | Grow followers | Expand reach to new audiences who may eventually book |

**Approach:** Authentic, nurturing, educational - not purely salesy. Content should build trust first, which naturally drives bookings.

## Target Audience

**Primary:** People actively considering an ayahuasca retreat
- Typically 25-55 years old
- Seeking healing from trauma, depression, anxiety, addiction, or seeking personal growth
- May have heard about ayahuasca through podcasts, books (Gabor Mate), or word of mouth
- Researching retreat options, comparing safety, authenticity, reviews, and price

**Secondary:** People curious about plant medicine / personal transformation
- Not yet committed but open-minded
- Follow wellness, spirituality, and personal development content
- Need education and trust-building before they'll consider booking

**Mid-tier competitors:**
- @ayahealingretreats (50K followers) - Consistent posting cadence
- @gaia.sagrada.ayahuasca (49K followers) - Impressive follower-to-post ratio
- @ayahuasca.colombia (19K followers) - Same market (Colombia)

**LaWayra's opportunity:** Most competitors either have polished but impersonal content (Rhythmia) or authentic but low-production content (smaller retreats). There's a gap for authentic + well-produced content with a clear booking pathway.

## Strategic Framework

Using Alex Hormozi's organic marketing frameworks as the backbone:
- **Core Four** (from 100M Leads): Content as one of four lead generation pillars
- **Hook > Retain > Reward** content structure
- **Volume + Consistency** as the growth engine
- **Value Equation** for offer positioning
- **Ad Assembly Process** (Hook > Meat > CTA) applied to organic content

## Strategy Decisions (Phase 2 Output)

### Content Pillars (4 pillars, 3-4 posts/week)
| Pillar | Purpose | Frequency |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Educate & Warn** | Reach new audiences via education + warning-framed Reels | 1-2x/week |
| **Show & Immerse** | POV tours, ceremony prep, facility B-roll Reels | 1x/week |
| **Prove & Trust** | Testimonials, review compilations, before/after | 1x/week |
| **Story & Connect** | Behind-scenes, shaman's story, founder journey (Stories + occasional Reel) | Stories daily when possible |

### Production Model
- **Solo creator** — one person films, edits, posts
- **Batch filming** — 2-3 hour weekly session producing 4-6 Reels
- **Hook multiplication** — film multiple hooks for the same meat content (1 shoot = 2-3 publishable Reels)
- **Reel structure** — Hormozi's Hook (0-3s) > Meat (3-20s) > Reward + CTA (20-30s)

### Awareness Level Content Mix
- 30% Problem Aware (depression/anxiety/feeling stuck — biggest untapped audience)
- 30% Solution Aware (ayahuasca as a healing path)
- 20% Product Aware (why LaWayra specifically)
- 10% Most Aware (direct booking CTAs)
- 10% Unaware (pattern interrupts)

### 90-Day Growth Targets
| Phase | Weeks | Cadence | Reel View Target |
|-------|-------|---------|-----------------|
| Launch | 1-4 | 3x/week | 1K+ avg |
| Optimize | 5-8 | 4x/week | 3K+ avg |
| Scale | 9-12 | 5x/week | 5K+ avg |
