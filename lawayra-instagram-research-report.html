Section 04

Hormozi Content Framework

Three interlocking frameworks from Hormozi's playbooks, applied specifically to LaWayra's Instagram strategy.

Content Unit: Hook → Retain → Reward

PhaseWhat It DoesLaWayra Execution
Hook (1-3 sec) Stops the scroll. Makes them say "this is for me." Bold text overlay + face (shaman or founder) + verbal hook from formula bank
Retain (middle) Keeps them watching. Uses curiosity & unresolved questions. Lists ("5 things..."), Steps ("The 3-phase process..."), or Stories ("She came carrying 20 years of grief...")
Reward (end) Delivers the value promised. Creates satisfaction + desire for more. Genuine insight, emotional payoff, or practical takeaway. Optional soft CTA.

Volume Rule

Section 05

Key Opportunities

Seven strategic gaps LaWayra can exploit to differentiate and accelerate growth.

01
Add a Personal Brand Layer
Model: @ayahuasca.colombia (Nico Canon)
Personal brands generate 5-20x more engagement than faceless retreat content. Feature the shaman, the founder, or a consistent face. People book people, not logos. LaWayra already has authentic indigenous shamans and a Gabor Mate association to leverage.
02
Bold, Hook-First Content
Model: All top performers
Every Reel over 20K views in this niche has a bold text overlay on frame 1. Adding bold text hooks to every Reel could immediately 5-10x view counts. This is the single highest-ROI change LaWayra can make.
03
Warning/Caution Content
Model: @ayahuasca.colombia (393K Reel)
"Who should NOT come to LaWayra," "3 red flags in ayahuasca retreats," "What most retreats won't tell you." Warning content builds trust by showing the brand cares about safety more than sales.
04
POV Facility Tours
Model: @ayahealingretreats (220K Reel)
Film an immersive cinematic walkthrough of the Fredonia location. Arrival, rooms, ceremony space, nature, meal preparation. Highest viral ceiling with lowest creative effort. Add trending audio.
05
Problem-Aware Audience
Gap: No competitor does this well
Create content for people who don't know ayahuasca is an option. Topics: depression, anxiety, feeling stuck, trauma, addiction. "Why talk therapy isn't enough for some people." This audience is 100x larger than the aya-curious market.
06
Own "Colombia" Positioning
Gap: Keyword under-utilized
Lean into Colombia as a destination. Cost, accessibility, natural beauty of Fredonia, Medellin proximity. "Why Colombia is becoming the #1 destination for plant medicine." Own this geographic niche.
07
Weaponize 500+ Five-Star Reviews
Unique advantage: No competitor has this volume of proof
2,500+ lives transformed and 500+ 5-star reviews is LaWayra's most powerful differentiator. Create "parade of proof" Reels showing rapid-fire review quotes. Pin a social proof post. Weave review snippets into every piece of content. This is the moat that no competitor can replicate quickly.
