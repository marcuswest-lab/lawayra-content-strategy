## Grains & Textures

Three textures are documented in the updated guide:

1. **Animated Film Grain** - Used as a background texture when you want a subtle visual layer behind content.

2. **Textile-Like Grain** - Used extensively on background images throughout the website. Serves two purposes:
   - Pushes the image into the background visually
   - Adds a rustic, spiritual energy/vibe to the design

3. **Design Element Grain** - Used sparingly, only on specific design elements (not backgrounds).

> **Purpose of documenting these:** Any visual creator given this branding guide should be able to produce work within these parameters and rules.
