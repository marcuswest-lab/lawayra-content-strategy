Section 01

Content Pillars

Four pillars that cover the full buyer journey — from "never heard of ayahuasca" to "ready to book."

⚠
Educate & Warn
Reach new audiences, build authority, go viral. Warning/educational Reels get 10-20x more views than any other content type. This is LaWayra's growth engine.
1-2x / week Reels (face-to-camera)
  • "Watch this before your first ayahuasca ceremony"
  • "How ayahuasca actually works in the brain"
  • "3 red flags that expose a fake retreat"
▷
Show & Immerse
Let people experience the retreat before booking. POV facility tours hit 220K views for a 49K-follower competitor. Highest viral ceiling with the lowest creative effort.
1x / week Reels (POV tours, B-roll)
  • POV walkthrough: arrival to ceremony space
  • Day in the life at LaWayra (sunrise to sunset)
  • The ceremony space at night (atmospheric)
★
Prove & Trust
Convert consideration into bookings through social proof. 500+ 5-star reviews and 2,500+ lives transformed are massively underused. "Parade of proof" compilations get 10-15K views.
1x / week Reels (testimonials) + Carousels
  • "What people are saying about LaWayra" (rapid-fire clips)
  • "Why 2,500+ people chose LaWayra" (carousel)
  • DM screenshots from past guests
♡
Story & Connect
Humanize the brand, build emotional connection. This is the "10x" in the 10:1 give-to-ask ratio. Casual, unpolished, human content that makes followers feel like insiders.
Daily Stories Stories + occasional Reel
  • The shaman's morning ritual
  • Preparing the ceremony space
  • "Why we started LaWayra" (origin story)
Pillar Balance
Educate & Warn is the volume driver. It should represent 40-50% of all feed posts. Show & Immerse and Prove & Trust each get ~25% of feed posts. Story & Connect lives primarily in Stories (daily), with an occasional Reel when a story is powerful enough for the full hook-meat-reward treatment.

Weekly Batch Filming Protocol

Hormozi Multiplication Method
Film ONE meat section, then record 3 different hooks for it. One shoot produces 3 publishable Reels. This is how you scale from 3/week to 5/week without additional filming time.

Section 04

Visual Standards

Non-negotiable visual rules based on research: 100% of top-performing Reels in this niche use bold text overlays on frame 1.

Text Overlay Rules Every Reel
  • Font: Bold sans-serif (Impact, Montserrat Black, or IG built-in bold)
  • Color: White with black outline/shadow (maximum contrast)
  • Size: Large enough for phone thumbnail (40-60% of frame width)
  • Position: Upper third of frame (visible in grid preview)
  • Case: ALL CAPS for hook text
  • Word limit: 8-10 words per overlay maximum
Thumbnail Formula 3 Elements
  • Element 1 — Bold Text: The hook from the overlay column in the Hook Bank
  • Element 2 — A Face: The creator, the shaman, or a guest
  • Element 3 — A Setting: Jungle, ceremony space, or retreat grounds
Color Grading: Warm/golden tones preferred. Rich greens for nature. Avoid cool/blue tones, heavy filters, or overly polished corporate looks. Light color correction only — the goal is authentic, not cinematic.

The Compound Effect
Content compounds. Every Reel is a permanent asset working 24/7. A Reel posted in Week 2 can go viral in Week 10. By Week 12, LaWayra will have 45+ permanent content assets on Instagram — each one a potential doorway for new guests to discover the retreat. The goal is not any single viral post. The goal is a system that consistently generates inquiries.

Review monthly. MORE of what works, LESS of what doesn't. Update the hook bank accordingly.
